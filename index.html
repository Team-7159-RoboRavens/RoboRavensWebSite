A FIRST® Tech Challenge Team Focused On Inspiration

FTC Team 7159, the Robo Ravens, is based in San Diego, California. Founded in 2014, Robo Ravens is the official FIRST® Tech Challenge team of Canyon Crest Academy. Open to all middle and high school students, our team has inspired hundreds to pursue STEM. With an award-winning outreach team, we are determined to foster the next generation of scientists and engineers.
